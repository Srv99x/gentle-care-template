A modern, minimal, and professional single-page website for a dental clinic with a soft blue and teal color palette, rounded UI elements, and smooth animations.

## Design System
- **Colors**: Soft blue and teal palette with plenty of white space
- **Style**: Clean, minimal, medical-themed with rounded corners and subtle shadows
- **Typography**: Modern, professional font hierarchy
- **Animations**: Smooth fade-in and scale effects on scroll

## Page Sections (Single Page Layout)

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Sticky header with clinic logo/name placeholder
- Smooth-scroll navigation links to each section
- "Book Appointment" CTA button in the header
- Mobile hamburger menu

### 2. Hero Section
- Clinic name and tagline: *"Caring for Your Healthy Smile"*
- Two CTA buttons: **Book Appointment** and **Contact Us**
- Clean medical/dentist illustration placeholder
- Soft gradient background

### 3. About Section
- Brief clinic description
- Highlight cards for mission, experience, and patient-first approach
- Stats (e.g., years of experience, patients served, doctors)

### 4. Services Section
- Grid of service cards with icons:
  - General Checkup, Teeth Cleaning, Dental Implants, Orthodontics, Emergency Care
- Each card with icon, title, and short description
- Hover animations

### 5. Doctors / Team Section
- Doctor profile cards with image placeholders
- Name, specialization, and years of experience
- Clean card layout with subtle hover effects

### 6. Why Choose Us Section
- Four highlight cards: Modern Equipment, Experienced Doctors, Affordable Care, Friendly Environment
- Icons and brief descriptions for each

### 7. Appointment Booking Section
- Simple form: Name, Phone, Date, Service dropdown
- Clean card-style layout with a submit button
- Client-side only (no backend)

### 8. Testimonials Section
- Patient review cards with name, photo placeholder, rating stars, and quote
- Clean horizontal card layout

### 9. Contact Section
- Address, phone, and email placeholders
- Google Maps embed placeholder (gray box)
- Two-column layout (info + map)

### 10. Footer
- Social media icon links
- Quick navigation links
- Copyright text

## Responsiveness
- Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile
- Mobile-friendly navigation with hamburger menu
- Stacked layouts on smaller screens
